History

DiamondCut Motors was founded in 1990 in Auto City, CA, with a focus on exceptional customer service and quality. We opened our first showroom in 1995 and expanded nationally by 2000. In 2005, we launched our website to improve accessibility. The "Diamond Standard Service" program began in 2010, providing personalized and lifetime maintenance services. In 2015, we partnered with top brands to offer exclusive models. We celebrated 30 years in 2020 with a flagship showroom in New York City, and in 2023, we launched our mobile app for seamless access to our services.
